Ham and Cheese

A classic fast food sandwich made at home with ham, cheese, and a spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of deli ham
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp hot sauce

Recipe

  1. Mix together mayonnaise, dijon mustard, and hot sauce.
  2. Butter the inside of each bun and toast in a pan or toaster.
  3. Place 2 slices of ham on each bun, followed by a slice of cheese.
  4. Spread the sauce on the cheese and top with the other half of the bun.
  5. Cook in a pan over medium heat until cheese is melted and bread is toasted.
